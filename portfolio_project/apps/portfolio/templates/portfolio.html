01 — Who I Am

About Me

Hey there! I'm Prashant Pal, a Certified Cyber Security Expert and B.Tech CSE graduate from Sir Padampat Singhania University, Udaipur (CGPA 7.0). I hold elite credentials including CEH v13 (AI-Augmented), CISSP, and CompTIA Security+.

I interned as a Cybersecurity Research Intern at IIT Patna, where I built an automated Attack Detection system using ML/DL achieving 80% accuracy against real-world DDoS and SQLi patterns. Most recently I worked as a Research Analyst at Arcgate Technologies, maintaining a 99.5% data accuracy rating across global tech clients.

My toolkit spans penetration testing (Metasploit, Nmap, Hydra), network analysis (Wireshark, Snort), and full-stack development (Python/Django, Nginx, Gunicorn). When not hacking or coding, I'm exploring Linux distros, hardware, or a good book.
